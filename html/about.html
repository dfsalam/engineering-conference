ROCKET GLOBAL SUMMIT 2023

The Global Summit brings together the community of experts, academics, and activist who comprise the Rocket Industry network in a different country every two years. This year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organization and individuals.

The Global Summit brings together the community of experts, academics, and activist who comprise the Rocket Industry network in a different country every two years. This year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organization and individuals who want to work with us on shared projects that contribute to expand the interest in rockets. So if you are modelist, engineer, technician or industry fan, we hope you will consider joining us this year to build a stronger community.
